Learning Computer Basics (L.C.B)

In a world where data is processed with a push of a button. Where everything is done mostly by machines and automatons. In an era where people are more active in social media rather than their family affairs. In a time when a phone or computer is more reliable when it comes to gathering information than a book. The only problem is how are we supposed to manipulate these so-called devices, for us to access the data we needed. That’s when we came up with an idea that will help a person of all ages, learn how to use a computer properly. L.C.B or Learning Computer Basics. A website that will guide a person through the basics of using a computer. Teach him or her how to use a computer properly, from the parts to proper posture and basic elements of a computer. This can also be used as a presentation for those who are less knowledgeable in using a computer. To teach those who are both young and old. Allowing them to learn with ease
